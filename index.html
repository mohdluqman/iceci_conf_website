About ICECI

International Conference on Emerging Computational Intelligence (ICECI) is organized in a hybrid format at Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. Organized by the Department of Computer Science, the first ICECI was successfully held on February 11-12, 2023. The second edition is scheduled for February 22-23, 2025.

ICECI aims to bring together researchers from around the world to discuss and explore advancements in areas such as Optimization, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Intelligence, Machine Learning, Evolutionary Computation, Cyber Security, Fuzzy Logic, Signal Processing Theory and Methods, Blockchain, Cloud Computing, Fog Computing, the Internet of Things (IoT), Wireless Sensor Networks, and their applications in various fields, including healthcare and disease management. Contributions that apply Machine Learning and Computational Intelligence techniques to other domains are also highly encouraged.

Department of Computer Science

Department of Computer Science, AMU is one of the oldest and pioneer departments of Computer Science in India. It came into existence in the year 1971 with the installation of IBM1130 Computing System, under the umbrella of Computer Centre and later blossomed into an independent department known as "Department of Computer Science" in the year 1988. Presently, this department offers various courses like Ph.D., M.C.A., M.Sc. Cyber Security & digital forensics, P.G.E.D.P., P.G.D.C.P. and B.Sc. (Hons.) Computer Applications. The syllabi for these courses are designed by keeping under consideration the requirement of time as well as the demand of the IT industry. Students from both undergraduate and postgraduate courses have been able to secure a place in IT companies, like TCS, Accenture, Infosys, IBM, JK Techno soft, Info system, NIC, Wipro, Bharti Telesoft, and many more. For more information, you may visit Department's website.

Aligarh Muslim University

Aligarh Muslim University is a public central university in Aligarh, U.P., India. It was originally established by Sir Syed Ahmad Khan as the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College in 1875. Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College became Aligarh Muslim University in 1920, following the Aligarh Muslim University Act. The university campus spans 1155* Acres Campus Area in the city of Aligarh, located about 130 kms away from New Delhi, the capital of India. At present, the university now boasts almost 39,367 students and 1,678 academic staff. It has 13 faculties comprising a wide spectrum of academic disciplines, 117 departments, 13 centers and offers about 325 courses. The University has laboratories equipped with modern and sophisticated instruments to encourage quality research.

AMU ranked among top ten Indian Universities in the 2021 NIRF India Rankings. Paper Submission

  • 1. The paper (pdf file) must be submitted electronically through Microsoft CMT.

  • 2. Paper must be in the given format with a minimum of 8 pages and a maximum of 12 pages.

  • 3. Authors are encouraged to submit mathematical, empirical and experimental research work only.

  • 4. The abstracts and manuscripts will be uploaded by the authors on or before the deadlines.

  • 5. The plagiarism must be less than 10%.

  • 6. All papers will be reviewed by at least three experts using a double-blind review process.

  • 7. All accepted papers must be presented by one of the authors.

  • 8. TPC committee will initiate the review process and further intimate the final outcome to the authors in due time.

  • 9. All accepted and presented papers will be published in the Scopus Indexed proceedings.

  • 10. The extended version of selected papers will be invited to submit and be considered for SCIE-indexed journals.
