About ICECI

The International Conference on Emerging Computation Intelligence (ICECI 2022) will be held at Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India, organized by the Department of Computer Science, on December 17-18, 2022. This conference is intended for researchers around the globe interested in the areas(not limited to) of Optimization, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Intelligence, Machine Learning, Evolutionary Computation, Fuzzy Logic, Signal Processing Theory and its methods, Blockchain, Cloud Computing, Fog Computing, the Internet of Things, Wireless Sensor Networks, various disorders and diseases, etc. The applications of Machine Learning and Computational Intelligence techniques in other domains are also welcome.

Department of Computer Science

Department of Computer Science, AMU is one of the oldest and pioneer departments of Computer Science in India. It came into existence in the year 1971 with the installation of IBM1130 Computing System, under the umbrella of Computer Centre and later blossomed into an independent department known as "Department of Computer Science" in the year 1988. Presently, this department offers various courses like Ph.D., M.C.A., P.G.E.D.P., P.G.D.C.P. and B.Sc. (Hons.) Computer Applications. The syllabi for these courses are designed by keeping under consideration the requirement of time as well as the demand of the IT industry. Students from both undergraduate and postgraduate courses have been able to secure a place in IT companies, like TCS, Accenture, Infosys, IBM, JK Techno soft, Info system, NIC, Wipro, Bharti Telesoft, and many more. For more information, you may visit Department's website .

Aligarh Muslim University

Aligarh Muslim University is a public central university in Aligarh, U.P., India. It was originally established by Sir Syed Ahmad Khan as the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College in 1875. Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College became Aligarh Muslim University in 1920, following the Aligarh Muslim University Act. The university campus spans 1155* Acres Campus Area in the city of Aligarh, located about 130 kms away from New Delhi, the capital of India. At present, the university now boasts almost 39,367 students and 1,678 academic staff. It has 13 faculties comprising a wide spectrum of academic disciplines, 117 departments, 13 centers and offers about 325 courses. The University has laboratories equipped with modern and sophisticated instruments to encourage quality research.

AMU ranked among top ten Indian Universities in the 2021 NIRF India Rankings. For the second year running, AMU finds a place in the 2021 ARWU (Academic Ranking of World Universities, also called Shanghai Ranking) list which contains only 14 topmost Indian institutions. Among Universities in India, AMU ranked fourth in India Today Ranking 2021. Among Universities in India, AMU ranked eighth in CWUR (Centre for World University Rankings) World University Rankings 2021-22 released in April 2021. In the subject-specific ranking results of US News & World Report in its 7th Annual Survey released in December 2020, for the subject "Biology and BioChemistry", AMU ranked first among all institutions and universities in India. For the subject "Mathematics", AMU is the only institution which has been ranked in India. Among Universities in India, AMU ranked fourth overall by US News & World Report in its 7th Annual Best Global Universities Rankings released in December 2020.AMU finds a place for the first time ever in the 2020 ARWU (Academic Ranking of World Universities, also called Shanghai Ranking) topmost universities list which contains only 15 topmost Indian Institutions. Among Universities in India, "Life Sciences" of AMU ranked first in Times Higher Education, London World University Ranking 2020 for subject "Life Sciences". Among Universities in India, "Physical Sciences" of AMU ranked second in Times Higher Education, London World University Ranking 2020 for subject "Physical Sciences".

Publication and Indexing

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research manuscripts in the areas (not limited to) of Optimization, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Intelligence, Machine Learning, Evolutionary Computation, Fuzzy Logic, Signal Processing Theory and its methods, Blockchain, Cloud Computing, Fog Computing, the Internet of Things, Wireless Sensor Networks, various disorders and diseases, etc. Submissions must be made through Easychair. All papers will be reviewed by at least three experts using a double-blind review process. Manuscripts for full papers should not exceed 12 pages using the Springer conference template, including references, figures, and tables. All papers must be submitted electronically in PDF format. All accepted papers must be presented by one of the authors to be included in the proceedings and published in the LNEE (Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering). The selected papers will be invited to submit an extended version of the paper to be considered for SCOPUS and SCIE-indexed journals.

Paper Submission

  • 1. Guidelines for authors can be found here
  • 2. Prepare your paper based on Springer Full Details
  • 3. Submit your paper online using ICECI 2022 Easychair account.(TBD)

  • 4. The submission and review process for ICECI 2022 will be done through EasyChair, an online manuscript management system. The abstracts and manuscripts will be uploaded by the authors using their EasyChair account on or before the deadlines. The authors can also check the status of their manuscript during review process.

  • 5. Once the manuscript submission deadline is over, the scientific committee will initiate the review process and further intimate the final outcome to the authors in time. All the manuscripts will pass through a double-blind review process from anonymous reviewers to be managed through the EasyChair publication system. For any problem encountered during review process, author can contact us.
